Disability Inclusivity

What is disability inclusivity? And how can I help?

The inclusion of people with disability is crucial in society because about 61 milion Americans live with a disability. It is highly important to present individuals with disabilities with the same oppurtunities as others. Employment rates among people with disabilities are noticeably low and children with disabilities often deal with discrimination at school. You can help by spreading awareness to combat ableism and by advocating for more safe and inclusive workplaces for people with disabilities.
